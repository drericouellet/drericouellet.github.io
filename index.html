Dr. Eric Ouellet

Senior Scientist | Non-Viral Intracellular Delivery

About Me

Dr. Eric Ouellet is a Senior Scientist at STEMCELL Technologies, specializing in non-viral intracellular delivery of nucleic acids and proteins into hard-to-transfect primary human cells. He led the development of the CellPore™ Transfection System, a next-generation microfluidic platform enabling high-efficiency delivery into immune and stem cells.

His work bridges engineering and life sciences, focusing on mechanoporation, electroporation, and lipid-based transfection. Dr. Ouellet has extensive experience with gene editing in T cells, NK cells, B cells, CD34+ hematopoietic stem and progenitor cells, and human pluripotent stem cells.

He also contributes to platform-agnostic product pipelines, aptamer development, and microfluidics innovation for intracellular delivery technologies.
